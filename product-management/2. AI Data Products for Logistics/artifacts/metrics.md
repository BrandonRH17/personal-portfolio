## Outcome Metrics (Business Impact)

| Metric | Before | After | Impact |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| **Tools used by operations team** | 4-5 disconnected tools | 1 unified CRM platform | **Single source of truth** |
| **Time to assign unit to shipment** | 15-20 minutes (manual lookup) | 2-3 minutes (CRM with availability view) | **~85% reduction** |
| **Client support calls for status updates** | ~30 calls/day | ~5 calls/day (self-service + WhatsApp) | **~83% reduction** |
| **Invoice generation time** | 30-45 minutes (manual) | 2-3 minutes (AI-automated) | **~90% reduction** |
| **Pilot follow-up response time** | Hours (manual WhatsApp) | Minutes (AI agent with escalation) | **Consistent follow-up** |
| **Fleet maintenance tracking** | Paper-based, delayed | Real-time digital, proactive | **Full visibility** |
