## Project Summaries

### 1. Data Product Management for US Retail Analytics

Led the data product function for a US-based post-purchase satisfaction analytics company, managing an 8-person offshore engineering team and collaborating with a solutions architect. Over 12 months, drove the migration from external data virtualization (Denodo) to an in-house data lakehouse on Databricks, built self-service reporting tools for client executives, and delivered six-figure annual cost savings while expanding the company's analytics offering.

**Focus:** Data product roadmap, offshore team leadership, platform migration, self-service reporting
**Highlights:** 8 offshore engineers managed, Denodo dependency removed, Databricks lakehouse with medallion architecture, six-figure cost reduction
